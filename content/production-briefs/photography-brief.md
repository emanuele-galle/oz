## Art Direction

### Mood & Aesthetic

**NON vogliamo:**
- ❌ Catalog photography (prodotto su sfondo bianco sterile)
- ❌ Lifestyle generico (modelli sorridenti, scene troppo staged)
- ❌ Over-produced (photoshop pesante, irrealismo)

**VOGLIAMO:**
- ✅ **Editorial luxury** (Vogue, Harper's Bazaar style)
- ✅ **Moody & dramatic** (chiaroscuro, contrasti, shadows)
- ✅ **Authentic** (momenti reali, emozioni vere, non fake)
- ✅ **Artisan** (focus su craft, dettagli, texture, mani che creano)

### Lighting

**Preferenze:**
- Natural light quando possibile (golden hour, soft morning light)
- Rim lighting per drama (controluce con fill sottile)
- Chiaroscuro per depth (non flat lighting mai)
- Hard shadows OK (non sempre soft — drama è luxury)

### Composition

**Rules:**
- Rule of thirds (quasi sempre)
- Negative space generoso (whitespace = luxury)
- Depth (foreground-midground-background layering)
- Asymmetry (non tutto centrato — più interessante)

### Styling & Props

**Color palette:**
- Gold accents (brand color)
- Nero/Midnight (backgrounds drammatici)
- Cream/Bianco sporco (soft backgrounds)
- Evitare colori saturi bright (solo toni muted/desaturati)

**Props minimali:**
- Focus su prodotto/persone, non props
- Se props: texture luxury (seta, velluto, marmo, vetro)
- Evitare props cheap/plastic

**Location:**
- Studio per product shots controlled
- Outdoor solo per specific lifestyle (golden hour, urban chic, nature)

## Technical Specifications

### Camera & Equipment

**Camera:** Full-frame (Canon R5, Sony A7, Nikon Z preferred)
**Lenses:**
- 24-70mm f/2.8 (versatile, product + portraits)
- 85mm f/1.4 OR 50mm f/1.2 (portraits, shallow DOF)
- 100mm macro f/2.8 (detail shots)

**Lighting:**
- Softbox large (2x) per product
- Beauty dish per portraits
- Reflectors (gold, silver, white)
- Optional: RGB LED per accent color effects

### File Specs

**Format:** RAW + JPEG
**Resolution:** Minimum 6000x4000px (24MP+)
**Color space:** Adobe RGB
**Post-production:** Color grading luxury (muted tones, film-like)

### Deliverables Format

**Per ogni shot:**
- RAW file (backup)
- High-res JPEG (full size, 300dpi, per stampa)
- Web-optimized JPEG (2000px wide, 72dpi, sRGB)
- WebP version (2000px wide, quality 85%)

**Organization:**
```
/raw/
  /product/cristallo/hero-1.CR3
  /product/scintilla/hero-1.CR3
  ...
/edited/
  /product/cristallo/hero-1.jpg (high-res)
  /product/cristallo/hero-1-web.jpg (web)
  /product/cristallo/hero-1.webp
  ...
```

## Mood Board References

### Photography Inspiration

**Le Labo Product Photography:**
- Minimal backgrounds (white/black/concrete)
- Dramatic shadows
- Focus su texture bottiglia, liquid visibility
- Macro dettagli (label, cap, spray nozzle)

**Byredo Lifestyle Imagery:**
- Scandinavian minimalism
- Muted color palette (desaturated)
- Unexpected compositions (not cliché)
- Models in motion (not static poses)

**Jo Malone Editorial:**
- Flat lays eleganti (not busy)
- Ingredient storytelling (raw materials + bottle)
- Soft romantic lighting
- Cream/white backgrounds dominant

**OZ Extrait Unique Touch:**
- Più italiano (marble, Verona architecture, Mediterranean light)
- Più audace (Zoe personality shines through)
- Più intimo (show the making, not just result)

### Color Grading Reference

**Look desiderato:**
- **Shadows:** Crush blacks leggermente (drama)
- **Highlights:** Soft rolloff (no blown whites)
- **Midtones:** Warm (gold undertone subtle)
- **Saturation:** -10% to -20% (muted luxury, non vivid commerciale)
- **Contrast:** Medium-high (non flat, non eccessivo)

**Film stock emulation:** Kodak Portra 400 look (warm, film grain sottile OK)

## Approval Process

**Shot approval workflow:**

1. **Pre-shoot:** Photographer condivide mood board + test lighting setup photos
2. **On-shoot:** Share 5-10 shots durante shooting per approval direction
3. **Post-shoot:** Photographer condivide ALL shots (RAW converted to JPEG preview)
4. **Selection:** Cliente seleziona 87 finali per editing
5. **First edit:** Photographer consegna first pass color grading
6. **Revision:** Max 2 revision rounds per shot
7. **Final delivery:** High-res + web versions

**Approval turnaround:** Max 48h per ogni step (per rispettare timeline Week 2)

## Contingency Plan: No Professional Photographer

**Se budget non permette professional photographer:**

### Alternative 1: AI-Generated Product Shots

**Tool:** Midjourney V6 OR DALL-E 3
**Prompt example:**
```
"Luxury perfume bottle on black marble, dramatic rim lighting,
product photography, editorial style, high-end cosmetics,
moody atmosphere, 8k, professional studio lighting --ar 3:4 --v 6"
```

**Pros:** €0 cost, infinite iterations, consistent style
**Cons:** Non fotorealistico 100%, etico discutibile, limitato per process shots

### Alternative 2: Stock Photography Curated

**Source:** Unsplash, Pexels (royalty-free) OR Adobe Stock (paid)
**Budget:** €200-500 per licenses
**Strategy:**
- Cerca "luxury perfume" "fragrance bottle" "artisan process"
- Filter per aesthetic alignment (moody, editorial, luxury)
- Curate 50-80 migliori
- Color grade consistently per brand alignment

**Pros:** Low cost, professional quality, immediate availability
**Cons:** Non exclusive, not branded, generic

### Alternative 3: Smartphone Photography DIY

**Setup:**
- iPhone 15 Pro OR Samsung S24 Ultra (have good cameras)
- Natural light (golden hour mandatory)
- Minimal props luxury (marble slab, silk fabric)
- Tripod per stability

**Learning:** YouTube tutorial "luxury product photography iPhone"

**Pros:** €0 cost
**Cons:** Richiede tempo, learning curve, qualità inferiore

**Raccomandazione:** Se budget <€1,500 → Stock photography curated + AI for unique shots è best compromise.
