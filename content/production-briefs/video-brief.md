## Technical Specifications Video

### Camera & Equipment

**Camera:** Cinema camera (RED, ARRI, BlackMagic preferred) OR high-end mirrorless (Sony A7S, Canon R5C)

**Lenses:**
- 24-70mm f/2.8 (versatile)
- 85mm f/1.4 (shallow DOF, cinematic)
- 100mm macro f/2.8 (detail shots liquid, drops)

**Frame rate:**
- 24fps (cinematic standard) per narrative
- 120fps (slow-motion) per detail shots (drops, mist, hands)

**Resolution:** 4K minimum (3840x2160)

**Additional gear:**
- Slider/dolly per smooth camera movements
- Gimbal/stabilizer per handheld smooth
- RGB LED lights (per accent colors)
- Smoke machine (per mist/atmosphere shots)

### File Specs

**Master files:**
- Format: ProRes 422 HQ (editing) OR H.265 (delivery)
- Resolution: 4K (3840x2160)
- Frame rate: 24fps final
- Color space: Rec.709 (web) OR DCI-P3 (if targeting premium displays)

**Web delivery:**
- MP4 (H.264 codec, high profile)
- Resolution variants:
  - 1080p (1920x1080) for desktop
  - 720p (1280x720) for mobile fallback
- Bitrate: 8-12 Mbps (quality-size balance)

## Post-Production & Color Grading

### Color Grading Look

**Target aesthetic:** Film-like, luxury, muted elegance

**LUT/Look Reference:**
- Kodak Portra 400 (warm, filmic)
- Fuji Pro 400H (muted, pastel-ish)
- Custom gold undertone (allinea con brand color)

**Grading specifics:**
- **Blacks:** Crushed leggermente (no pure black, lift a 5-10%)
- **Highlights:** Soft rolloff (protect gold tones from blowing)
- **Skin tones:** Warm (rosato-dorato, no yellow-green)
- **Saturation:** -15% globally (luxury muted, non vivid)
- **Grain:** Film grain 3-5% (texture, organic feel)

### Editing Style

**Pacing:**
- Slow (luxury non ha fretta)
- Cuts: Max 8-12 per 30s video (average 2.5-3s per shot)
- Transitions: Crossfade (1-2s), NO whip pans, NO zoom effects

**Text overlays:**
- Font: Cinzel (headings) + Inter (body)
- Color: Gold OR white con shadow
- Animation: Fade in/out (1s), NO slide/bounce
- Placement: Rule of thirds (non sempre centrato)
